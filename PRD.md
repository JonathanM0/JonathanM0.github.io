## 4. User Stories
<!-- 
Who are the different visitors to the website, and how do they use the website?
-->
- **As a peer**, I want to view the work to see different styles and techniques to get inspiration/reference.  
- **As a potential employer**, I want to look for examples of the persons work to demonstrate skills.  
- **As a layman**, I want to look at visuals/projects that are appealing or interesting and maybe even look to commission something. 
- **As a social media user**, I want to see photos of finished or in progress work to pieces ive potentially found online .  

## 5. Design 
<!-- 
Include basic design and user experience considerations. You can include color schemes, fonts, layouts, etc.
-->
- **Brand aesthetic** Minimal, modern aesthetic with clean typography and ample white space.  
- **Color Scheme:** Mostly white with cyan and black text 
- **Typography:** Futura for headings, frutiger for body text.  
- **Navigation:** Top menu bar with links to Home (default page), Illustrations, Animations, Design, other, and About me.  
- **Layout:** Simple, single-column scrolling pages with sections clearly separated and works displayed with descriptions side by side.  
- **Images:** High-quality photos of work such as character turnarounds or storyboards etc, videos of animations and motion graphics.
